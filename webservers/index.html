Antibody Engineering

CSM-AB

: A web-server for accurate antibody-antigen binding affinity (KD, ∆G) predictions.

Be aware of:

  • This web-server does not do anything with “Docking” but single point energy calcualtion; thus, users should provide their query antibody-antigen complexes to get their binding affinity predictions done.
  • Binding affinity, KD (Dissociation Constant), represents how strong the antibody or antigen binds to its partner. In this study, we simply used ∆G for “binding energy”.

What’s the advantages of using CSM-AB?

  • Better performance and easier to use than other available tools
  • Very fast and efficiently re-rank the docked ab-ag complexes

mCSM-AB2 and mmCSM-AB

: Predicting the changes of antibody-antigen binding affinity (∆KD, ∆∆G) upon single point mutation (mCSM-AB2) and multiple mutations (mmCSM-AB)

Be aware of:

  • Binding affinity, KD (Dissociation Constant), represents how strong the antibody or antigen binds to its partner. In this study, we simply used ∆∆G for the changes of “binding energy” upon mutation.
  • The negative ∆∆G represents decreasing of ab-ag binding affinity and vice versa. This is opposite to the conventional sign of ∆∆G because we calculated the labels (∆∆G) by ∆G(WT) - ∆G(MT).

What’s the advantages of using mCSM-AB2 and mmCSM-AB?

  • Better performance and easier to use than other available tools
  • Very fast and efficiently browse hotspots or meaningful mutations via Alanine Scanning or Saturation Mutagenesis.

Pharmacokinetics and Toxicity

Deep-PK

: A web-server for pharmacokinetics and toxicity screening, interpretation and optimization.

Be aware of:

  • The 73 pharmacokinetics and toxicity models each have their own chemical space of training molecules. Thus, the confidence for distant or unique query molecules can be low. Take a look of model’s performance and confidence levels in result page.

What’s the advantages of using Deep-PK?

  • Better performance and easier to use than other available tools
